# research-experimental
Hypothesis-driven research scratchpad: super early-stage exploration of ideas that may or may not become portfolio projects

> "I wonder if X is true."

A place where I can test early-stage explorations of ideas, signals, and hypotheses that sit outside my structured curriculum. Driven by hypotheses, cross-disciplinary academic theory, etc.

---

## What lives here

| Folder | Contents |
|---|---|
| `technology-megatrends/` | Exploring technological adoption patterns and market signals |
| `alternative-data/` | Satellite, NLP, and non-traditional data source exploration |
| `advanced-topics/` | Techniques and ideas ahead of my current curriculum stage |
| `signal-research/` | Early-stage factor and signal ideas, pre-rigour |

## What distinguishes this from learning experiments

Each subfolder here starts with a hypothesis or research motivation — even if brief.
